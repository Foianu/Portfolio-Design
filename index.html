My name is Valentina, I work in Design Industry for 7 years; I have worked for 4 years in 3D Architectural Design and Graphic Design.

My free time was filled up with modeling for my Portfolio, but also modeling some hard surface projects for Gaming Industry.

Over the years I have discovered Illustration Design and I managed to combine it with my Graphic Design skills and that is how " Music Simulation Collection " was born.

I also expanded my vision and designed a few UI/UX design prototypes, which led me to learn Web Design. With this experiment I created some websites.

Learning new programs is my way of playing games.

Last year another experiment got my attention, I disassembled old phones for fun and I thought to start a new project. It is called tablou.tech and it became a passion and a business.

I am currently working as a Freelancer and provide design services.

Being comfortable with using Photoshop, for my previous years of photo editing, I really started learning Graphic Design 7 years ago, at the same time I was learning 3D modeling.

Between 2020 and 2022, working from home, I had more energy and no additional project, so I learned Illustration Design. My firsts drafts were illustrated coloring books, mandalas, and logos.

Last year, looking for a new way to present my work, I thought it would be a great idea to blend my illustrations with Packaging Design.

My goal here was to find a suitable solution to change my approach to showing my skills in a clear and easily detectable way.

My long-term work in progress project, " Music Simulation Collection " is a way of creating a product.

My Voxel Collection was a project I started a few years ago because I wanted to strengthen my modeling skills and discover other hidden ideas.

It was my favorite 3D project to work on because I had the freedom to create some stories.

My favorite things as a child were building houses out of plasticine, playing Sims just to decorate the houses, doing puzzles, and drawing. So my 4-year job was like a dream come true. From there I was able to expand my creative abilities, trying new things.

Working in 3D Architectural Design Industry, I started to like Interior Design too. So I worked on my portfolio and played with my ideas.

I was curious about each object I introduced into my scene, this made me start learning the structure of each object.

I have been learning, modeling, creating, building, iterating for 4 years, but my journey on the job respecting the requirements.
